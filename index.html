HOW IT WORKS

Once a week, together

1

Start together

You both hold a button to begin. Same room, same moment. It takes about 15–20 minutes.

2

Answer privately

You each answer the same questions on your own screen. Your partner can't see what you're writing until you're both done.

3

Reveal at the same time

Answers appear simultaneously. No editing after the fact. You see what each of you actually thought — not what you thought the other wanted to hear.

4

Make commitments

After the reveal, you decide together: what to do this week, what to keep doing, and what to let go of. Kindred brings those commitments back next week.
